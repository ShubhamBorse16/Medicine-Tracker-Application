Medicine Tracking Application

About the Application

The Medicine Tracking Application is a powerful tool that facilitates the management and tracking of medicines and medical stores. It is designed to provide seamless communication between users and medical stores, ensuring a smooth experience for both parties.

Admin Approval for Medical Stores

The application is equipped with an Admin interface that allows the administrator to approve and manage medical stores. When a new medical store registers on the platform, the admin reviews the details and, if satisfied, approves the store. This approval process ensures that only legitimate and trusted medical stores are listed on the application.

Search for Medicine

Users can easily search for specific medicines using the application's search feature. They can input the name of the medicine they need, and the application will display the relevant medicines available in various medical stores. This feature simplifies the process of finding the required medicine and saves time for the user.

Location of Medical Stores

In addition to providing information about available medicines, the application also offers the location of each listed medical store. Users can access the address and location of the nearest medical store where the required medicine is available. The integration of maps ensures that users can easily navigate to the selected medical store.

Benefits of Medicine Tracking Application

  • Efficient management of medicines and medical stores
  • Streamlined communication between users and medical stores
  • Quick search for required medicines
  • Real-time tracking of medicine availability
  • Reliable and trusted medical stores approved by the admin
  • Convenient navigation to medical stores using map integration
